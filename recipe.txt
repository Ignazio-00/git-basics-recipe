Eggs, Salt, Pepper
Truffle-Pasta

Ingredients:

- 2 eggs
- 2 tablespoons of butter
- 2 tablespoons of milk
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat lightly.

2. Add the butter to a small nonstick frying pan over medium heat.

3. Add the eggs to the pan, stirring constantly for about two minutes or 
until the egg mixture looks wet but not runny.

4. Reduce to low heat and add the milk, stirring until the eggs become 
creamy and cooked through.

5. Season with salt and pepper as desired.

6. Serve hot. Enjoy!
